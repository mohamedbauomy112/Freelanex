Token Values


DOM tokens are used to pay for all activities on the platform, such as:

  • Creation of Assets
  • Trading of Assets
  • Creation of support tickets
  • Voting for charities
  • Listing a property for sale
  • Listing a property for rent
  • Registering a rental agreement
  • Registering a purchase agreement

Fees charged on DOMINIUM are charged in EURO, but can only be paid in DOM tokens:

  • €0,25 for each support ticket creation
  • €0,50 to vote for a charity
  • €1,00 to buy an Asset
  • €1,00 to sell an Asset
  • €1,00 to list a property
  • €2,50 to register a rental agreement
  • €5,00 to register a purchase agreement
  • €500 to create a non-regulated Asset (exempt)
  • €2,500 to create a regulated Asset

Token Burn Program

All DOM tokens collected on DOMINIUM and acquired via the buy-back program will be collected in a multi-signatory account, of which the company supervisor, Confidon, will be a signatory. These tokens will therefore be taken out of circulation upon deposit in the account and will be burned (destroyed) every year, or as required. Token burns will be audited and publicly announced.

This makes the DOM token one of the few tokens with a diminishing supply!!!
